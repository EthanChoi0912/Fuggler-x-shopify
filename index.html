Conclusion結論

The plan moves the store from fugglerstore.com to shop.fuggler.com with a clean, on-brand Shopify launch in August 2026, through a disciplined validation window on SHEIN-backed fulfilment, into a paid and organic growth push across Q4 2026, and finally an international expansion year in 2027. Each phase carries one testable goal, so Libertas can sign off on outcomes rather than activity. 本計劃將商店由 fugglerstore.com 遷移至 shop.fuggler.com，於 2026 年 8 月以品牌一致的 Shopify 形態上線；隨後進入基於 SHEIN 履約的驗證期、2026 年第四季度的付費與自然增長階段，並於 2027 年完成國際擴張。每階段均設有可驗證的目標，便於 Libertas 以結果而非活動進行驗收。

Critical dependencies — clean 301 redirect map from fugglerstore.com, SHEIN inventory API readiness, Shopify Markets setup for the UK / EU / US, GA4 and Shopify analytics governance, and a content calendar aligned with the Fuggler brand voice on the hub site. 關鍵依賴 — 由 fugglerstore.com 完整遷移的 301 重定向清單、SHEIN 庫存 API 就緒、Shopify Markets（英／歐／美）配置、GA4 與 Shopify 數據治理，以及與 Fuggler 母站品牌語調一致的內容日曆。

Recommended next step — confirm Phase 1 scope (domain migration cut-over date, design sign-off and SHEIN integration spec) by mid-July 2026 so the August go-live remains on the critical path. Quantitative targets will be set jointly with Libertas after the first four weeks of live GA4 data, rather than committed to upfront without historical baselines. 建議下一步 — 於 2026 年 7 月中前確認第一階段範圍（域名切換日期、設計定稿與 SHEIN 對接規格），確保 8 月上線處於關鍵路徑。具體量化目標將於上線首四週 GA4 數據沉澱後與 Libertas 共同設定，而非在無歷史基線情況下預先承諾。
